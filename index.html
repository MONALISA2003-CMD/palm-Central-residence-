About the Development

Where the sea meets
a new definition of home

Positioned on Frond N of Palm Jebel Ali, Palm Central Private Residences is not simply an address — it is a considered way of living. Every elevation captures the Arabian Gulf, and every interior reflects a commitment to quality that goes beyond the visible.

Launched on 22 June 2026 by Nakheel, the development arrives as Palm Jebel Ali establishes itself as Dubai's most ambitious waterfront district. One to five bedroom residences range from intimate coastal retreats to expansive family homes, each finished to a standard that respects the discernment of those who will call them home.
